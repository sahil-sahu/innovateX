Check Laptop IP Address

Follow these steps to check the IP address of your laptop:

  1. Click on the Wi-Fi icon in the system tray or taskbar.
  2. Select your Wi-Fi network from the list of available networks.
  3. Click on "Properties" or "Advanced settings" (depending on your operating system).
  4. Look for the "IPv4 Address" or "IP Address" field in the network settings.
  5. Note down the IP address displayed.
  6. Place it on the app input box

Once you have the IP address, you can use it for various purposes, such as connecting to local servers, troubleshooting network issues, or accessing network resources.
